Paneer Tikka Masala





Source of Recipe : Paneer Tikka Masala by Ranveer Brar

Description

About the dish

Char-grilled cottage cheese simmered in a velvety tomato and cashew reduction, delicately infused with smoked spices and finished with fresh cream. A timeless North Indian indulgence.

History

Paneer Tikka Masala originated in North India, where grilled paneer tikka was combined with a rich and creamy tomato gravy. Inspired by traditional Punjabi flavors, it has become one of the most loved Indian dishes worldwide.

Ingredients

For Gravy

  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 2 Dry red chillies
  • 2 Green cardamom
  • 4 Medium-sized tomatoes, cut in halves
  • Half inch of ginger slice
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves
  • 2 medium sized White onion, cut in halves
  • Half tsp turmeric powder
  • 1.5 tsp Deggi/Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Corriander powder
  • 2 cups of Water
  • Salt as per taste
  • 6 Cashew nuts

For Marination

  • 1 tsp Deggi/Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • Quarter tsp Garam masala
  • A pinch of asafoetida (heeng)
  • 1.5 tbsp Mustard Oil
  • Salt as per taste
  • Half tbsp Ginger Garlic paste
  • Half cup Hung curd
  • Half tbsp Roasted Gram flour
  • 400 gms Paneer, cut into medium size cube

For Onion-Tomato Masala

  • 1 tbsp Ghee
  • 2 tbsp Butter, cubed
  • 2-3 Bay leaf
  • 1 large Onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Degi red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • Half tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 large Tomato, finely chopped
  • Prepared Paneer Tikka
  • Prepared Gravy
  • Quarter cup fresh Cream

Others

  • Coal
  • Butter, cubed
  • 1 tbsp Ghee

For Garnish

  • Coriander sprig
  • fresh Cream

Steps

For Gravy

  1. In a deep pot or kadai, add oil once the oil is hot, add dry red chillies, green cardamom.
  2. Add tomatoes, ginger, garlic and saute on medium flame. Add white onion saute well.
  3. Add turmeric powder, degi red chilli powder, coriander powder saute on medium flame for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Add water and mix well. Add salt to taste, cover it with the lid and cook on medium flame for 5-7 minutes until the tomatoes get soft.
  5. Add cashewnut and give it a boil. Once it is cooldown, transfer it into a grinder jar and make a smooth paste of it. Keep aside for further use.

For marination

  1. In a bowl, add degi red chilli powder, garam masala, asafoetida, mustard oil, salt to taste.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste, hung curd, roasted gram flour, paneer and mix everything well.

For Tikka

  1. In a shallow non-stick pan, add ghee once it is hot, roast the prepared paneer tikka on the pan.
  2. Basting with ghee and cook from all sides.
  3. Transfer the cooked tikka to the plate and keep aside for further use.

For Onion Tomato Masala

  1. In a shallow, add ghee, butter once it is hot, add bay leaf, onion and saute well.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste, degi red chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder saute well.
  3. Add tomato and saute it well for 4-5 minutes. Add prepared paneer tikka, prepared gravy and give a nice stir.
  4. Cook for 2-4 minutes on low flame. Finish it with fresh cream.
  5. In a small bowl, add coal, butter and cover it with the lid for a few minutes to give a nice smokey flavor to the gravy.
  6. Remove the coal aside, transfer the paneer tikka masala into a serving bowl, garnish it with coriander sprig, fresh cream and serve hot with roti or paratha.
